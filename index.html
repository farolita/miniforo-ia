FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Mini AI Forum? ▼
An intimate gathering of tech experts and business leaders in Montevideo, Uruguay, organized by Fabián Kopel to explore how AI is transforming industries. Limited to ~32 participants for meaningful conversation.
Who can attend? ▼
The forum is by invitation only. It brings together CEOs, founders, and AI specialists from Uruguay and the region to foster direct, high-quality exchanges.
How can I contact Fabián? ▼
You can reach Fabián Kopel through LinkedIn or via the contact form on the Kopel Sánchez website. He is always open to connecting with people passionate about AI and technology.
What is the ToolBox? ▼
A curated collection of AI tools and resources shared by forum participants. When someone shares a URL in the Foro section, it is automatically added to the ToolBox with an AI-generated description.
